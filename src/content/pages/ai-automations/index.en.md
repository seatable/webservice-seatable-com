    - name: faq

      title: FAQs – AI automation with SeaTable
      items:
          - q: What does AI-supported automation mean at SeaTable?
            a: 'AI-powered automation in SeaTable means that **intelligent functions such as summarization, classification, extraction, and OCR** are available directly as actions in automations. Instead of executing individual prompts in external tools that are connected to your systems via workflows in tools such as n8n or make and API, SeaTable lets you create everything in one system, ensuring maximum transparency and control. This allows you to manage your processes efficiently, design lean AI-based process automation, and reduce manual work and technical effort—all in one system.'
          - q: How does the integrated SeaTable AI work?
            a: SeaTable hosts the multimodal model Gemma-3-12B on its own server in the German Hetzner data center in Frankfurt. Your AI automation transfers the relevant content to the model in encrypted form, and the AI writes the results back to your SeaTable base in a structured format. 
          - q: Can we use our own AI models?
            a: SeaTable Server customers can use the [SeaTable AI component](https://admin.seatable.com/installation/advanced/seatable-ai-standalone/) to connect different LLMs and providers via an OpenAI-compatible API for their AI automations. You can choose between self-hosted models or cloud GPTs. SeaTable Cloud users are currently unable to connect their own models.  
          - q: Is our data protected in accordance with the GDPR when using AI?
            a: Absolutely, protecting your data is a priority at SeaTable, and we place great importance on ensuring that all AI processes are GDPR-compliant. That's why we host our own AI model on German servers. Your data is processed exclusively within the SeaTable infrastructure and is not passed on to external providers or used to train external AI systems. If you process particularly sensitive data or have to comply with very strict data protection requirements, you can alternatively run SeaTable with AI functionality completely on-premises.
          - q: Is the use of the AI feature in SeaTable included in the subscription?
            a: Yes, the AI feature can be used at no additional cost. However, if you make a lot of AI queries, additional costs may apply. Depending on the subscription model, each SeaTable user receives a certain monthly quota of free AI credits, which are required to use AI in automations. Additional costs are only incurred if credits are required beyond this quota. 
          - q: Can I audit what AI automation is used for?
            a: Absolutely. Transparency about your AI process is a key advantage of SeaTable's integrated AI features. As a team admin, you can see in SeaTable team management which automation with AI actions has used how many credits. This allows you to develop and control AI policies and budgets in a targeted manner.
          - q: What is an AI no-code solution?
            a: An AI no-code solution such as SeaTable is a platform that allows business users or citizen developers to use AI functions and build automated workflows without having to program.Typical features include visual interfaces such as drag-and-drop editors, predefined actions for AI automations such as classification, text recognition, or summarization, and the integration of AI automation into visual automation editors.
