With a large number of bases, it can become quite confusing on your homepage. Therefore, use SeaTable's practical search function to find bases quickly.

![Search for a base](images/search-for-a-base.gif)

- Open the **Homepage** of SeaTable.
- Use the **key combination** {{< key "Ctrl" >}} + {{< key "F" >}} or click in the **search field** in the top right corner.
- Select whether you want to search your **bases** or your **apps**.
- Now enter a **part of the name** of the base you are looking for in the search field – and SeaTable will provide you with all matching **search results**.
- Click on the **name** in the hit list to open the desired base.

{{< warning  headline="Notice"  text="You can currently only search by name, not by content within a base." />}}

You will also see your last search results in a **search history** and can access these bases directly.

![Last searched base](images/base-last-searched.png)
